About Me

Who I am and what I do
Hello, my name is Robert Chen, but you can call me Bobby. I am a fledgling developer, coffee lover, gaming enthusiast, swimmer turned water polo player and a hard worker who is deeply passionate about everything I do.

Who am I?
I am a fun-loving and friendly person. I greatly enjoy creating polished products. I am always excited to learn new things. I am a dedicated worker who is also a great team player. I love solving challenges and I love competition. Both bring out the best in me.
Where I am in my life?
I am Computer Science major studying at Amherst College. I was a pre-medical student until the end of my Junior year (completed the MCAT and everything!), when I decided that path just wasn't for me. But that's a story for another time; I am now looking to pursue a career in Computer Science. I've yet to truly experience the field, and I look forward to the prospect of exploring it as much as I can.
What are my hobbies?
My favorite sports are Competitive Swimming and Water Polo, and I've participated in both for years. I'm no good at land sports, and that is unfortunately not a joke. I love painting, although I've only recently begun my journey into that world. I am an avid gamer, and my first steps into the world of Computer Science were from high school, when I wrote programs to assist my gameplay and contributed code and content to gaming websites. I find satisfaction in improving myself by learning new skills, fixing mistakes, and matching myself up against others.
What are other fun facts about me?
I am a black coffee enthusiast (but mochas are a guilty pleasure). I love history. My favorite work of literature is Antigone. My favorite movie is The Prestige. I like long walks in Nature, and shorter walks through busling cities. I've never been able to wrap my head around magic tricks.
